About Us

"Flip & Match" is a game created with the intention of bringing people together in a spirit of friendly competition and enjoyment. Whether you're playing with family, friends, or even strangers, the game offers a level playing field where everyone has an equal chance to succeed. Our aim is to provide a source of entertainment that fosters social interaction and cognitive development.

Rules

Rule of the Game: The rules of "Match & Flip" are simple yet engaging. Players are presented with a grid of facedown cards, each depicting different images, symbols, or numbers. The objective is to flip over pairs of cards to find matching items. Here's how to play:


1.Setup: Shuffle the deck of cards and arrange them facedown in a grid formation on the playing surface.


2.Turn-taking: Players take turns flipping over two cards at a time, aiming to reveal matching pairs. The order of play can be determined by mutual agreement or by random selection.


3.Matching: If a player flips over two cards that match (i.e., they have the same image, symbol, or number), they keep the pair and earn a point. The matched cards are removed from the grid, leaving empty spaces.


4.Non-matching: If the cards flipped by a player do not match, they must be turned facedown again, and it becomes the next player's turn.


5.Memory: As players continue to flip cards, they must rely on their memory to remember the location of previously revealed cards. This adds an element of strategy and challenge to the game.


6.Winning: The game continues until all pairs of cards have been matched. The player with the most pairs (and points) at the end of the game is declared the winner.


7."Match & Flip" offers endless possibilities for variation and customization. Players can choose different themes for the cards, adjust the grid size, or introduce additional challenges such as time limits or special abilities.


So gather your friends and family, sharpen your memory skills, and get ready for a thrilling game of "Match & Flip"!
